The Best Trip I Have Ever Taken (So Far!)

In the Spring of 2019, my best friends and I decided to take a two week trip to explore Japan! Additionally, I had a friend living in Melbourne, Australia at the time so I thought it would be perfect to continue my overseas trip to visit her! (I was going to be in the same hemisphere after all). After months of planning, my husband, my best friend from grade school and a close college friend embarked on a trip of a life time!

Tokyo, Japan

The first stop on our journey was the quintessential Japanese city, Tokyo! We explored many different neighborhoods and ate so much amazing food!

Takayama, Japan

Our next stop was a trip into the gorgeous Japanese Alps. There, we explored the ancient city of Sanmachi Suji.

We then took a bus trip further into the mountains to stay at a Japanese Ryokan and experience the healing properties of natural hotsprings and enjoy their rotemburos (outdoor baths) with beautiful views of the surrounding mountains.

Kyoto, Japan

Next stop, Kyoto! We were able to explore beautiful temples, shrines and bamboo groves. My travel partners and I took in a Japanese culture show and learned how to participate in a traditional Japanese tea ceremony. We also rocked the house AGAIN in a private Karaoke suite!

Osaka, Japan

Thanks to Japan's amazing public transport, we were able to take a day trip from Kyoto to visit the port city of Osaka. We explored the numerous fish markets and sampled some of the freshest sushi we will ever have in our lives! We then got to explore the famous Osaka Castle, first built in 1580!

Back to Tokyo, Japan!

Even though we were sad our Japan trip was coming to a close, we still had a few more days to further explore Tokyo! We spent the rest of our time exploring a digital art museum, visiting a Kawaii Monster Cafe, eating plenty of sushi and singing our hearts out at more Karaoke bars!

Melbourne, Australia

After saying goodbye to my friends, I took a nine hour plane trip to Melbourne, Australia! I explored the city with my best friend from college, shopped at thrift stores and the famous Woolworths supermarket.

Airlie Beach, Australia

We then flew up the east cost of Australia to Airlie Beach to explore a plethra of gorgeous beaches and snorkel the Great Barrier Reef! We took a day cruise to the Whitsunday Islands and took a snorkeling excursion.

Cedar Creek Falls

On one of our last days we swam in the pristine waters of Cedar Creek Falls and climbed to the top of the waterfall to take in the gorgeous views.

This trip was a life changing experience and I cannot wait to revisit all the amazing places we visited!
